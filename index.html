For our out of town guests, here are some tips on travel and accomodations!

     Flying to JFK


Taxi: $52 flat rate + tolls
Subway: Take the Airtrain to Howard Beach station, and then take the A train to Manhattan for around $7.75
Train: Take the Airtrain to Jamaica station, and take the LIRR to Penn Station for $15.

     Flying to Laguardia


Taxi: Around $50

     Flying to Newark


Taxi: Around $70 to Manhattan
Train: Take the Airtrain to NJ Transit, NJ Transit to New York Penn Station, ~$20

Uber is also a popular option for all of the above (and for getting around in the city).
